# Cognitive State Alert Template

## Purpose
Diagnose the cognitive state of market narratives and investment analysis to identify potential biases and improve decision-making quality.

## Theoretical Foundation

Based on the **Cognitive Variability Framework** from InfraNodus, which maps thinking patterns into four states based on idea diversity and connectivity.

- Seek out short seller reports

### If FOCUSED ðŸŸ¡

- Assign probabilities to alternative outcomes
- Identify the "what would change my mind" triggers

### If DIVERSIFIED ðŸŸ¢

- Focus on monitoring key variables
